About Markuss

I build analytics systems that hold up after the presentation ends: measured, testable, and tied to a real operating decision.

Background

How I Think

01
Find the constraint

The useful problem is usually the bottleneck limiting throughput, conversion, access, quality, or trust.

02
Make failure visible

Monitoring, provenance, audit trails, and explicit caveats matter as much as the successful output.

03
Test the claim

A model result or management narrative should survive an honest attempt to disprove it.

04
Ship the handoff

The work is not finished until another person can inspect it, use it, and understand the decision it supports.
